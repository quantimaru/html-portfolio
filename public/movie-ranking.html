Top 3 movies

Ironman (2008)

I think that almost everyone knows Ironman. I have chosen the first movie of the trilogy, because I love the "rebirth" of Tony Stark. At the same time I like the thought about becoming a superhero without any special superpowers, (almost) only intelligance.

Project Almanac (2015)

This movie is about time travelling. I love the concept how human emmotions, social priorities and egoism can ruin the whole space-time. Really recommend.

Asterix and Mission Cleopatra

This movie is my childhood. I love the french humor and I love the story.
